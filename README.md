This application serves as a smart faculty-locator system designed to help students—especially newcomers—quickly find teachers across various departments. The app organizes all departments, their courses, and corresponding faculty members in a clean and accessible dashboard. A universal search bar allows students to instantly search teachers by name, making it easier to identify where they are seated on campus.
Each faculty profile displays essential details such as room number, floor, department, and course, helping students navigate the campus without confusion. After logging in, authorized users or administrators can add new faculty members, edit existing details, or update location information, ensuring the database stays current and accurate.
The system is designed to be simple, intuitive, and fast, offering students a seamless way to explore academic staff, understand department structures, and locate teachers without manual guidance. Overall, the app enhances campus navigation and improves interaction between students and faculty by providing a reliable, structured information tool.
